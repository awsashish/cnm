The World On Bitcoin

Overview
Bitcoin was invented as a peer-to- peer system for online payments that does not require a trusted central authority. Since its inception in 2008, Bitcoin has grown into a technology, a currency, an investment vehicle, and a community of users. In this guide, i hope to explain what Bitcoin is, what are the views of some prominent people about it and how it works as well as describe how you can use it to improve your life.

Goals
To provide a collective feedback and views of some trusted and famous people on Bitcoin The inception of cryptocurrencies has laid a remarkable hand on the economy of the whole world, while bitcoin holds the prominent and robust place, all the other Cryptocurrencies drift and fade away. More and more people are embracing this cryptographic achievement and Bitcoin due to its fame and unrelenting surge will continue to exist forever.
